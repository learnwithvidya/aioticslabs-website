About AIOTICS Security Labs

AIOTICS Security Labs (ASL) is a deep-tech cybersecurity initiative focused on the intersection of tools, labs and training for IoT, OT, ICS and smart infrastructure. The objective is to make industrial and embedded security more accessible through compact scanners, OT toolkits, cyber range concepts and carefully structured educational experiences.
